# AAU Helpdesk Sample Data

This file contains sample data and documentation for the AAU Helpdesk Chatbot project.

## Sample Queries and Expected Responses

### Admission Inquiries
- "I want to apply for computer science admission"
- "What are the requirements for engineering program?"
- "How do I apply to medicine at AAU?"
- "When is the application deadline for business?"

### Registration Help
- "How do I register for second semester 2024?"
- "I need help with course registration"
- "What is the registration process?"
- "When does registration open for fall semester?"

### Fee Payment
- "I need to pay 5000 birr for tuition"
- "How do I pay university fees?"
- "What are the payment methods?"
- "Where can I pay my semester fee?"

### Document Requests
- "I need my transcript from engineering department"
- "How do I request a degree certificate?"
- "Can I get my grade report urgently?"
- "What is the process for transcript request?"

### Grade Inquiries
- "What are my grades for first semester 2023?"
- "I want to check my results"
- "How do I access my grades?"
- "When will grades be released?"

## Parameter Examples

### Departments
- Computer Science, Engineering, Medicine, Law, Business, Economics, Psychology, Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics, English, Amharic

### Semesters
- First, Second, Third, Fall, Spring, Summer

### Document Types
- Transcript, Certificate, Diploma, Degree, Grade Report, Academic Record, Student ID

### Years
- 2023, 2024, 2025

### Fee Amounts
- 2500, 3000, 5000, 7500, 10000 (in Ethiopian Birr)

## Intent Categories

1. **admission_inquiry** - Questions about university admission
2. **registration_help** - Course registration assistance
3. **fee_payment** - Payment-related queries
4. **transcript_request** - Document requests
5. **grade_inquiry** - Grade and result queries
6. **course_information** - Course and curriculum info
7. **schedule_inquiry** - Class schedule questions
8. **document_request** - General document requests
9. **general_info** - General university information
10. **technical_support** - Technical issues and support
